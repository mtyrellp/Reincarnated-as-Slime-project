"I am not a bad slime"

"Veldoraa"

Upon being reborn as lowly Rimuru awakened inside of a mysterious cave and was attacked by many different monsters such as Giant Snakes, Spiders, and Centipedes in the mist of this he meets a Dragon long sealed inside the cave. In there he makes friends with the Storm Dragon Known as Veldora. As they talk Velodra gives him the name Rimuru and Rimuru gives Veldora a family name of Tempest. After becoming friends Rimuru promises to help Veldora escape from his prision and stores Veldora in his slime stomach to analyze the seal on him. After escaping from the cave he founded a town he would soon call Tempest.
